About

Michael Siewert

I’m Signature Mike, a familiar face in Augusta, Georgia. Since moving here from Washington, DC in 2005, I’ve built and grown my interior design business, Signature Interiors, into a trusted name for custom interior design and space planning.

At Signature Interiors, my team and I work with clients locally and around the world. We specialize in new home construction, full home renovations, luxury redecorating, and both residential and commercial interior design projects. Whether you’re building from the ground up or refreshing an existing space, I create interiors that feel personal, timeless, and thoughtfully designed.

The Signature Mike Show is a 30-minute lifestyle and design program airing every Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday at 3:00 PM on WRDW-TV. Each episode brings together helpful interior design ideas, creative crafts, local guests, community stories, and practical solutions to everyday design dilemmas.

On the Show

Design tips, creative crafts, special guests, and real community stories.

Informative, fun, and full of personality, The Signature Mike Show brings design, lifestyle, and community together in every episode. Viewers can enjoy practical home inspiration, creative ideas, conversations with local businesses and non-profits, and moments with friends and family of Signature Mike.

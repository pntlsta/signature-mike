About

Michael Siewert

Interior Designer

Signature Mike is a familiar face in Augusta, Georgia. His business, Signature Interiors, has continued growing since moving there in 2005 from Washington DC. The store is packed with furniture, art, lamps, chandeliers, rugs, home decor and gifts.

Mike and his staff work with many clients around the globe, from new home construction to redecorating, residential or commercial. Mike writes a weekly design column for the Augusta Press. His weekday TV show also airs on YouTube.

Design Philosophy

Mike is fluent in every design language. He travels to markets, keeps up with trends, and uses vast resources to find the perfect fit for every client. The goal is a space that reflects the client’s lifestyle.

Awards

Mike has been recognized for his interior design work and has been voted #1 Interior Designer in Columbia County, GA for seven years.

Hands-On Design

He Does the Work Himself

Mike is very hands-on with every project he touches — and that's a money saver. Because he doesn't have to hire as many contractors, clients get more value for their investment. And Mike makes sure the job is done right, every time.

From hanging window treatments and installing hardware to arranging furniture and styling every detail, Mike is personally involved at every stage. That level of care is what sets Signature Interiors apart.

The Person Behind the Brand

Meet Mike

Whether he's on set filming The Signature Mike Show, visiting a client's home, or exploring the CSRA for his next Mike in Motion adventure, Michael Siewert brings the same energy and creativity to everything he does. Design isn't just his business — it's who he is.

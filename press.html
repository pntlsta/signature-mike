The Signature Mike Show

Airing every Tuesday, Wednesday & Thursday at 3 PM on WRDW-TV (CBS Channel 12, Augusta, Georgia), The Signature Mike Show is a 30-minute lifestyle and interior design program hosted by Michael Siewert. Each episode brings together practical design ideas, creative crafts, community stories, local business features, and Signature Mike's on-location adventures across the CSRA.

For press inquiries or interview requests, contact Mike@SignatureMike.com.

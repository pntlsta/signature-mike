May 2026 Highlights

A month's worth of design inspiration, community stories, on-location adventures, and moments from The Signature Mike Show — airing every Tuesday, Wednesday & Thursday at 3 PM on WRDW-TV in Augusta, Georgia.

8 Episode Highlights
May 2026
Watch on YouTube
Episode 2131
Outdoor Living

The Perfect Outdoor Refresh

Mike tackled one of May's most timely topics: transforming outdoor spaces just in time for warm-weather entertaining. He walked viewers through simple ways to elevate a patio or backyard corner using layered textiles, statement planters, and strategic lighting — all without a major renovation budget. This segment is a go-to reference every spring.

Watch this moment
Design Dilemma

Small Space, Big Personality

Right out of the gate in this episode, Mike dove into one of his favorite challenges: making a compact room feel spacious and intentional. He covered the rules he breaks, the tricks he swears by (hello, mirrors and vertical lines), and why "small" is never a limitation — just an invitation to be more creative.

Mike in Motion

On Location in the CSRA

Near the end of this episode, Mike took the show out of the studio and into the community for one of his on-location Mike in Motion segments. He explored a beautifully designed local space, pointed out what made it work, and shared the kind of design eye you only develop by getting out and looking at spaces in real life. One of May's standout moments.

Watch this moment
Creative Crafts

DIY That Actually Looks Good

Mike brought one of his hands-on craft segments to life this episode, showing viewers how to create a high-end-looking decorative piece without spending high-end money. His process was step-by-step, his commentary was entertaining as always, and the finished result was exactly the kind of thing you'd pin and actually make. Practical creativity at its best.

Watch the full episode
Episode 2135
Design Trends

What's Actually Trending in 2026

Mike broke down the interior design trends worth paying attention to in 2026 — and, more importantly, which ones to skip. From earthy tones and natural textures to the conversation around "color drenching" a room, this episode was a real-talk design briefing for anyone looking to refresh their space this year. Essential viewing.

Watch the full episode
Community Spotlight

Voices from Augusta

Mid-episode, Mike welcomed a guest from the Augusta community for a conversation that was equal parts inspiring and grounding. Whether it's a local nonprofit doing meaningful work or a business owner with a story worth sharing, these community spotlight segments are what make The Signature Mike Show feel like a genuine part of the CSRA — not just a design show.

Episode 2139
Room Transformation

Before & After: The Reveal

Episode 2139 delivered one of May's most satisfying moments: a full before-and-after room reveal. Mike walked through the thinking behind each design decision — how the layout changed, why certain pieces were chosen, and what makes the final result feel cohesive and personal. The kind of episode that reminds you why good design matters in everyday life.

Watch the full episode
Episode 2140
Design Tips

Mike's Top Takeaways for May

To close out the month, Episode 2140 gave viewers a focused burst of practical design wisdom. At about the ten-minute mark, Mike shared a set of actionable tips you can apply to any room right now — no contractor, no big budget, just a sharper eye and a few key moves. The perfect note to end May on and head into summer with a fresher, better-designed home.

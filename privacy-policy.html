Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters.

This Privacy Policy explains how Signature Mike may collect, use, and protect information submitted through this website.

Last updated: May 25, 2026

1. Who We Are

This website is operated for Signature Mike / Signature Interiors & Gifts, located at 3505 Professional Circle, Augusta, GA 30907. You may contact us by email at Mike@SignatureMike.com or by phone at 706-447-1503.

2. Information We May Collect

When you use this website, we may collect information you choose to provide, such as your name, email address, phone number, project address, project notes, selected contract option, and any message you send through a form.

The contract preparation tool is designed to help you create a printable contract. It does not need to submit your contract online unless a future website feature is added for online submissions.

3. How We Use Information

Information you provide may be used to respond to your inquiry, prepare project communication, help you create a printable contract, schedule a consultation, improve the website experience, or provide customer service.

4. Printable Contract Tool

The printable contract tool allows visitors to enter information and generate a contract preview for printing or saving as a PDF. Information entered into this tool is used to populate the printable contract shown on your screen.

Unless the website is later updated to include online submission, this information is not automatically sent through the website. Users are responsible for printing, saving, signing, and bringing the contract to the store.

5. Cookies and Website Analytics

This website may use basic cookies, analytics tools, or hosting platform features to understand website traffic, improve pages, and maintain site security. These tools may collect technical information such as browser type, device type, pages visited, general location, and time spent on the site.

6. Sharing Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only when needed to operate the website, respond to your request, provide services, comply with legal requirements, or work with trusted service providers who help support the website or business.

7. Third-Party Links

This website may link to third-party websites, social media platforms, video platforms, maps, or other external services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or policies of those third-party websites.

8. Data Security

We use reasonable steps to protect information submitted through the website. However, no website or online communication is completely secure. Please do not submit sensitive personal information through general website forms.

9. Children’s Privacy

This website is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact Signature Mike at Mike@SignatureMike.com or 706-447-1503.
